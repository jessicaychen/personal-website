Research Projects

Advanced Robotic Eye Surgery Lab
August 2019 - March 2020

Retinal Layer Segmentation Project

Used MATLAB to develop an algorithm to perform real-time image and data processing of OCT volume scans to extract the coordinate of a certain feature with user input, later to be implemented in a prototype for retinal robotic eye surgery. Utilized averaging of image planes, Gaussian blur, polynomial fitting, and removal of outliers to reduce image noise and optimize image segmentation.

  • Github Repository

Orbital Fat Volume Analysis

Ocular Motility Lab

August 2019 - December 2020

Manually segmented the orbit and intra-orbital contents in MRI volume scans of 44 subjects. Used ImageJ to batch process the images and calculate the orbital orbital fat volume in different sections. Used SPSS to analyze the difference in orbital fat between controls and patients with Primary Open Angle Glaucoma with regular intra-ocular pressure and had received prostaglandin treatment.

Choroidal Volume Analysis

Ocular Motility Lab

January 2019 - October 2019

Scanned 40 subjects’ eyes in different gaze positions and extracted optical coherence tomography (OCT) volume B-scans. Manually segmented the choroid in Materialise Mimics in young and old subject groups. Conducted statistical analyses of difference in volume reduction in SPSS. Documented results in a first-authored paper.
